    Featured animals

    Diesel

    Get up and get excited, because Diesel is in the house!  This under 3 year old black and white Pit Bull Terrier boy is a very sweet pup with loads of energy, and he cannot wait to meet you!  Diesel will need daily exercise and will make the ideal companion for hiking, running, and speed-walking. Get your tail to the shelter and take home a handsome fellow like Diesel, A076264! 

    Danny & Sandy

    They are perfect-sized little cuties that have adopted each other, and now just need to find someone to adopt THEM!  Danny, A077338, is a white and tan Chihuahua boy with awesome ears, coming in at around 2 years old.  Sandy, A077343, is his sister from another mister, sporting a pure white coat and representing the Poodle Miniature mixes.

    Bianca

    Beautiful Bianca is a white and black Domestic Longhair cat looking for a loving home to complement her gorgeous fur!  She is about 8 years old and is seeking a quiet life, where gazing out the window and being admired from the couch are among the daily activities. Don’t waste another moment; adopt Bianca, A077082! 

    Luna

    Pretty sure there’s not a prettier girl around than Luna the American Staffordshire Terrier. She is about 5-years-young and has the personality of a winner!  Luna will be a great canine buddy to older kids that understand her natural strength and excitement level. Luna, A075282, the prettiest of pups, is ready to make your acquaintance!
